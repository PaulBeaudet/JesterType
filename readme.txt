Gesture Based Text input device

General Goal discription
Produce a typing device that through intuitive interaction significantly reduces typing effort thus lowering barriers in computer interaction that assist practical, pleasant and meaningful communication. Underlying goals of the project are as follows.

Primary Goals
Finely polished process to learn how to used device. Less than 15 minutes to explain basic use, 1 hour to mutual* understanding of layout user/device understanding of layout and a week of regular use to adapt to significant muscle memory associated with speed and efficiency.
Have a more efficient feel than a traditional full layout keyboard.

Secondary Goals
provide a haptic form of signalling to the user to receive messages and suggestions.
Habit recognition as a secondary factor for computer access security, In combination with passwords
